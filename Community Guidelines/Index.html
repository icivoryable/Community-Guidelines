Tampa ICE Watch

Neighbors protecting neighbors. We share verified alerts to keep our families safe from deportation.

🛡️ We never ask for your real name or immigration status.

🤝 Our Community Agreements

1. We don't call the police.

Law enforcement puts undocumented neighbors at direct risk.

2. We protect privacy.

We never photograph neighbors' faces or civilian license plates.

3. We keep alerts secure.

We do not post rumors or alerts on public social media.

4. We act in solidarity.

We rely on everyday neighbors who step up to care for each other.

💬 Why we use Signal (Not WhatsApp)

ICE and other agencies are known to join public WhatsApp groups to gather info. We use Signal because it is fully encrypted and they cannot monitor our community conversations.

📱 How to join the network

  • 1
    Download the Signal app using the QR codes below. It's completely free.
  • 2
    Create an account. You can use a nickname or an alias to protect your privacy!
  • 3
    Hide your number: Inside the app, go to Settings > Privacy > Phone Number. Set "Who Can See My Number" to Nobody.
